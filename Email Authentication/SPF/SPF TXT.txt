The basic syntax of the SPF TXT record for a custom domain in Microsoft 365 is:
v=spf1 <valid mail sources> <enforcement rule>

v=spf1 [<ip4>|<ip6>:<PublicIPAddress1> 
<ip4>|<ip6>:<PublicIPAddress2>... 
<ip4>|<ip6>:<PublicIPAddressN>] 
[include:<DomainName1> 
include:<DomainName1>... 
include:<DomainNameN>] 
<-all | ~all>
